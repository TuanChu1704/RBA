Game Story

In this immersive game, you find yourself transported to the bustling campus of RMIT University's School of Science and Technology. Armed with your wits and a thirst for knowledge, you set out to explore the sprawling campus and learn the programming languages that will unlock its secrets.

As You Navigate The Real-World Map Of The SGS Campus, You Encounter Interactive Challenges That Test Your Skills In HTML, CSS, And C++. You Learn How To Create Dynamic Web Pages, Style Them With Python, And interact with computer hardware and software Using C++.

As you progress through the game, you unlock new areas of the campus and uncover hidden treasures that reward your programming prowess. You also meet other NPC like students and professors who share their insights and expertise, helping you to hone your skills and deepen your understanding of programming concepts.

With each new challenge you conquer, you gain confidence in your abilities and unlock new opportunities for learning and growth. Will you be able to master all the programming languages and emerge as the ultimate campus programmer? The choice is yours!
